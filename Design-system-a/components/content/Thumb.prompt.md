**Thumb** — the hatched image placeholder used in every card, row, and preview. A striped box + mono caption; never real or fake art.

```jsx
<Thumb ratio="4:5" caption="Instagram" />
<Thumb ratio="16:9" caption="preview" radius="0" />
```

- `ratio` accepts keywords (`1:1`, `4:5`, `4:3`, `16:9`, `poster`) or a raw CSS aspect-ratio.
