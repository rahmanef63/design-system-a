**Drawer** — a slide-over from the right (default) or left, with a titled header + CloseButton. The mobile form of RightPanel and the Notifications panel. Esc + backdrop dismiss.

```jsx
<Drawer open={open} onClose={close} title="Notifikasi">
  <EmptyState glyph="◔" body="Notifikasi Anda akan muncul di sini." />
</Drawer>
```
