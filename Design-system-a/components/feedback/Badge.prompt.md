**Badge** — the tiny mono label for status and category: Baru, Private, Beta, Gratis, Folder. Keep them short; they signal, they don't explain.

```jsx
<Badge tone="new">Baru</Badge>
<Badge icon="🔒">Private</Badge>
<Badge tone="highlight">★</Badge>
```

- `new` = accent fill (the "Baru" tag). `highlight` = the yellow annotation marker — use sparingly.
